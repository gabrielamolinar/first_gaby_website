ABOUT ME

Electronic Engineer, entrepreneur, Venezuelan, adopted by Germany

Welcome to my website. This page tries to describe me, my job and the things I do in my free time.

Watzmann, German Alps


I was born, grew up, and studied in Venezuela. My alma mater is the Universidad Simón Bolívar in Caracas. I studied there electronics engineering. During my studies, I did an exchange year at the Karlsruher Institut für Technologie in Germany.
I always wanted to learn other languages, see the world, and move to Europe. I did it. Now I am living in Germany from 2015.
I need to say, this place has received me with open arms. Since 2016 I am working on a PhD. My colleagues are excellent. Some of them are now friends of mine. We play DnD, board games, do sports and some times even cook together. Others are my entrepreneur partners. We discuss together (sometimes dreamy) ideas. Some of them are now successfully implemented!
In Germany, I have learned how to take care of myself, while staying focused at work, even at the most stressful times. Mindfulness, exercise, healthy eating, and sleeping well are just keywords for many, but they actually work, trust me.
I have discovered the Alps, and now I am in love with them.
